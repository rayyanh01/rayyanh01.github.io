Soccer

The Enver Creek Soccer Team is a group of hard-working, athletic, and intelligent students. They thrive for success and try their absolute hardest at every match they play. For any questions or concerns, please contact the EC Soccer coach for more details.

The Basketball team has a wide variety of athletic students who are very passionate about the sport. They work very hard during practice and in games and show off their Cougar pride. Please contact the Basketball coach for more information.
