About Enver Creek

Enver Creek Secondary is one of the best schools in all of British Columbia. It is located in Surrey, BC. Every single student is eager to learn and work in a safe environment. The school is filled a diverse group of students. We try our absolute hardest to make sure every student is learning in the most effective way possible.

Some of our goals are to help improve academic achievement by using the focus on grade 8 and 9 students success rate. Increase literacy skills, allowing students to read a variety of text for educational purposes. Finally to improve the mathematic success rate.
